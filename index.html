🚨 Emergency Triage — Unconscious Patient
▸ PATIENT IS DOWN — WHAT DO I DO?
1. Submerged / no oxygen?
YES →
Move to dry, oxygenated area. Diving Suit if pressurized.
NO →
Continue to step 2.
2. Active bleeding? (unstable — gets worse)
BLEEDING →
Bandage or Plastiseal immediately. Priority #1.
NO BLEED →
Continue to step 3.
3. Internal damage / lacerations?
YES →
Opium (mild) or Morphine (severe). Should restore consciousness.
MINIMAL →
Continue to step 4.
4. Oxygen Low primary affliction?
MEDIC 25+ →
Perform CPR while they have air access.
NOT MEDIC →
Stabilozine, Liquid Oxygenite, or Deusizine. Bad CPR = harm.
5. Severe blood loss?
YES →
Saline or Blood Pack. Ensure bleeding is stopped first.
MINOR →
Leave it — minor blood loss recovers on its own.
Last resort: Inject Anabolic Steroids (+200% max vitality) to buy time. Adrenaline forces brief consciousness.
🩺 Crew Medical — Afflictions & Treatment
TRIAGE: Bleeding O₂ Low Internal Burns / Other
⬤

Internal Damage / Lacerations / Bite & Gunshot Wounds

▶
Caused by: blunt impacts, falls, melee, creatures, bullets, sharp objects
  1. Check severity via Health Scanner HUD.
  2. If also bleeding → treat bleeding first.
  3. Opium for moderate (heals damage + minor burns).
  4. Morphine serious, Fentanyl critical.
  5. Watch for overdose — have Naloxone ready.
💡 20% damage + 20% burns = one Opium covers both. Don't waste bandages.
⬤

Bleeding

▶
Caused by: sharp weapons, bullets, creature bites
  1. Unstable — worsens over time, causes blood loss. Prioritize.
  2. Bandage minor, Plastiseal heavy bleeding.
  3. Antibiotic Glue also works (8 uses/tube).
  4. Minor bleeding heals on its own — don't waste supplies on trivial cuts.
⬤

Blood Loss

▶
Caused by: untreated bleeding
  1. Stop the bleed first — blood loss climbs while bleeding is active.
  2. Saline (−50/dose), Blood Pack (stronger), Alien Blood (substitute).
  3. ~10% blood loss? Leave it — they'll recover.
⬤

Burns

▶
Caused by: fire, welding, overvolted junction boxes, thermal artifacts
  1. Antibiotic Glue — best burn treatment.
  2. Bandage / Plastiseal also work.
  3. Opiates heal burns as secondary — covers damage + burns together.
  4. Lower priority than bleeding or internal. Heals slowly on its own.
💡 Hardmode: untreated burns → Infected Wounds. Treat promptly.
⬤

Oxygen Low / Suffocation

▶
Caused by: unconsciousness, heavy opiates, no O₂, submerged without mask
  1. Ensure patient has breathable air first.
  2. CPR with Medical 25+. Bad CPR = harm.
  3. Not medic? Stabilozine / Liquid Oxygenite / Deusizine.
  4. Often: treat underlying injuries → consciousness → they breathe on their own.
💡 Unconscious = stop breathing. Getting vitality above 0 is usually the best "O₂ treatment."
⬤

Husk Infection

▶
Caused by: husk / infected crawler bites, husk egg injection
  1. Detect early — Health Scanner HUD spots Stage 2.
  2. Early: Broad-Spectrum Antibiotics (multiple doses may be needed).
  3. Best: Calyxanide — all stages. 1–4 doses.
  4. LATE STAGE — Antibiotics cause damage. Calyxanide only.
  5. Can't cure? Lethal intervention — turned husks infect the crew.
💡 Last resort: Sufforin/Cyanide kills infection, but you must treat the poisoning immediately.
⬤

Opiate Overdose / Addiction

▶
Caused by: repeated Opium / Morphine / Fentanyl use
  1. Naloxone — only cure for overdose + addiction.
  2. Stabilozine helps in a pinch.
  3. Use weakest effective opiate. Don't Fentanyl minor injuries.
⬤

Psychosis

▶
Caused by: Deliriumine poisoning, exploring ruins
  1. Prevent: Ethanol / Pipe Tobacco → Psychosis Resistance.
  2. Fades once source removed. Keep affected crew away from critical systems.
⬤

Pressure Damage (Barotrauma)

▶
Caused by: outside without diving suit, flooded pressurized room
  1. NO MEDICATION CURE — must fix the environment.
  2. Get patient to pressurized dry area. Diving Suit outside (mask ≠ pressure protection).
  3. Fix hull breach or evacuate. Treat secondary injuries once safe.
⬤

Stun / Paralysis

▶
Caused by: stun baton/gun, chloral hydrate, creatures
  1. Wears off on its own (30s max).
  2. Adrenaline — instant removal, brief forced consciousness.
  3. Hyperzine / Methamphetamine shorten duration.
🔧 Submarine — Repair & Maintenance
REPAIR: Hull Breach Flooding Fire Elec / Mech
⬤

Hull Breach

▶
Caused by: creatures, explosions, collisions
  1. Diving Suit if heavily flooded.
  2. Locate: Status Monitor (orange/red) or listen for water.
  3. Welding Tool (needs fuel) — right-click aim, left-click weld.
  4. Weld inside first, then exterior armor.
  5. Activate Pumps to drain. Check corners/thruster areas for hidden leaks.
💡 Can't find it? Go outside, weld entire hull with a scooter. Higher Mechanical = faster.
⬤

Flooding (No Visible Breach)

▶
Caused by: open exterior doors, pump malfunction, ballast flora
  1. Status Monitor — trace the source.
  2. Seal all exterior doors/hatches.
  3. Check pumps — reverse "pump in." Destroy Ballast Flora if present.
  4. Pump from bottom up — water flows downward.
💡 Even partial flooding affects ascent — ballast tanks can't drain properly.
⬤

Fire

▶
Caused by: overvolted junction boxes, reactor, thermal artifacts, incendiaries
  1. Fire Extinguisher immediately.
  2. DANGER — fire eats O₂ fast. Mask up if needed.
  3. Move explosives / O₂ tanks — fire detonates them.
  4. Fix overvolted junction box or reduce reactor output. Treat crew burns after.
💡 Overvolted junctions = #1 fire cause. Keep reactor matched to demand.
⬤

Electrical Damage (Junction Boxes, Wiring)

▶
Caused by: overvoltage, flooding, deterioration, explosions
  1. Screwdriver → interact → hold repair.
  2. Wiring gone? Wire + Screwdriver → wiring interface → drag into power slot.
  3. Quick-fix: connect to any nearby powered device (even a light).
  4. Drain room first — submerged devices take continuous damage.
💡 Low skill rewiring = electrocution risk. Engineers repair dramatically faster.
⬤

Mechanical Damage (Pumps, Engines, Fabricators)

▶
Caused by: normal use, flooding, explosions, wear
  1. Wrench → interact → hold repair.
  2. Fabricator degrades through use — repair after crafting.
  3. 0% condition = inoperable. Don't let things deteriorate.
⬤

Reactor Overheat / Meltdown

▶
Caused by: excessive output, unmanaged fuel rods
  1. Reduce power — match to demand.
  2. Critical? Shut down reactor. Battery backup buys time.
  3. Reduce battery charge rate / disable non-essentials.
  4. Replace spent fuel rods.
  5. MELTDOWN = GAME OVER
💡 Auto-control works but is less efficient than manual management.

📦 Quick Reference
⬤

Tool → What It Fixes

▶
  1. Welding Tool → Hull breaches. Welds doors shut.
  2. Screwdriver → All electrical (junction boxes, wiring).
  3. Wrench → All mechanical (pumps, engines, fabricators).
  4. Crowbar → Force doors, floor vents.
  5. Plasma Cutter → Cut welded doors, salvage.
⬤

Medicine → What It Treats

▶
  1. Bandage / Plastiseal → Bleeding + Burns
  2. Antibiotic Glue → Burns + Bleeding + Infected Wounds (8 uses)
  3. Opium → Internal + burns (mild)
  4. Morphine → Internal + burns (moderate)
  5. Fentanyl → Internal + burns (critical) HIGH OD RISK
  6. Saline / Blood Pack / Alien Blood → Blood loss
  7. Naloxone → Opiate overdose + addiction
  8. Calyxanide → Husk (all stages)
  9. Broad-Spec Antibiotics → Husk (early) + Infected Wounds
  10. Stabilozine / Liq. Oxygenite → O₂ Low (non-medic CPR alt)
  11. Deusizine → Endgame multi (damage, blood loss, stun)
  12. Adrenaline → Instant stun removal, brief consciousness
